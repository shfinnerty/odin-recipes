California Burrito

The worlds best burrito, found only in Southern California...

Carne asada, guacamole, sour cream, cheese... all the fixin's for an incredible burrito plus, what's that? french fries?? Thats right. Alongside the savory grilled carne asada lies a bed of fresh crispy french fries. Drench this bad boy in salsa, and you're well on your way to taste bud heaven.

Ingredients:

  • Xtra large flour tortilla
  • Carne asada
  • Guacamole
  • Sour cream
  • French fries
  • Shredded cheddar jack cheese
  • Salsa

Steps:

  1. Grill your carne asada
  2. Warm the tortilla on a hot flat top
  3. Remove tortilla from flat top and begin loading on your ingredients. Add the carne first, then the cheese to allow the hot meat to melt the cheese a bit, then add all your other ingredients
  4. Fold in both ends of the tortilla and roll that badboy up
  5. Slice in half for easy eating, and drench that bitch in salsa
  6. Take a bite and enjoy your new life
