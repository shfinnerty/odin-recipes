Beer

Yes, I am counting it as a food!

There's nothing quite like an ice cold pint on a Friday evening after a long week of hard work (and coding!). Beer has been a staple in the human diet for about 5,000 years, and it's still just as good, better in fact, today! Cheers!

Ingredients:

  • Fermentables:
    • Pilsen dry malt extact
    • Pale malt 6 row
    • Munich malt
    • Flaked corn
    • Flaked barley
  • Hops
    • Northern brewer pellet
    • Saaz pellet
  • Yeast
    • Wyeast - Kolsch 2565

Steps:

  1. Add water to kettle and add dry malt extact
  2. Mash: Heat water to 16-F, submerge grains in bag. Mash for 1 hour.
  3. After an hour of mashing, pull grains out. You now have wort!.
  4. Bring the wort to a boil, stirring often. Add hops, boil for 60 minutes.
  5. After 60 minutes, cool the wort to 75F
  6. Siphon wort into fermenter, swirl vigorously, and add yeast.
  7. Move your fermenter to a dark, cool place
  8. Wait a few weeks, bottle it up, let rest in fridge for another week.
  9. Pull a few cold ones out of the fridge, and toss em' back with the boys!
